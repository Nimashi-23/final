About Me

A firm believer in marine conservation, I have spent the last five years traveling to underwater projects and promoting them across the globe. I believe the ocean is the lifeblood of this planet, and protecting its ecosystems is a prerequisite for a bright future.

I can use my marine biology background and on-the-field training to participate in various programs that protect the oceans. Specifically, I would like to work on coral reef restoration. In addition, I'm interested in providing pathways to marine biodiversity, with a future goal of a vibrant underwater ecosystem for the coming generations.
